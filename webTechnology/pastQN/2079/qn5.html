box-sizing: content-box;

(width and height only apply to the content of the element)

This div has a width of 400px, but the full width is 400px + 20px(left and right border)+40px(left and right padding) = 460px.


box-sizing: border-box;

(width and height apply to all the parts of element: content, padding and border)

Here, the full width of the box is 400px, including content, padding and borders
